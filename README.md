"Even Saving a Single Life is a Great Victory."












📖 Overview

CJACK is an AI-enabled wearable emergency response system designed to provide immediate automated CPR during Sudden Cardiac Arrest (SCA).

The system combines wearable hardware, biomedical sensing, intelligent control, emergency communication, and edge computing to deliver standardized chest compressions before professional medical responders arrive.

Unlike conventional mechanical CPR systems that are expensive and primarily used in hospitals, CJACK is designed to be portable, affordable, and suitable for deployment in public spaces.

Our vision is to make lifesaving CPR as accessible as a fire extinguisher.

❤️ Problem Statement

Current challenges in emergency cardiac care include:

Delayed CPR initiation
Lack of CPR awareness
Incorrect compression depth and rate
Rescuer fatigue
Limited emergency manpower
Lack of automated CPR devices in public places
💡 Our Solution

CJACK provides:

🤖 AI Voice Guidance
❤️ Automated CPR Assistance
📞 Automatic Emergency Calling
👕 Wearable Smart Jacket
📊 Real-Time Patient Monitoring
🌐 Edge AI & Multi-Device Integration
🎯 Vision

To develop an affordable, intelligent, wearable CPR system capable of providing immediate lifesaving assistance before professional medical responders arrive.
